# Video

A card layout featuring a media preview image with a call-to-action.

Used to guide readers to view a video on the web.

### Usage Note

Add a divider (bottom border) when a stronger separation between modules is required.

For example, add a divider when the media module appears above a bulleted list.

## Guidelines

-   Choose an effective poster image to represent your video
-   Ensure your poster image features a "play" icon or similar to indicate video media
